UX/UI Challenge

UX/UI challenge to design a home automation interface (6-8h).
Design a home automation interface Home automation refers to the automatic and electronic control of household features, activity, and appliances. Imagine a home where everything, from lighting, climate, air conditioning, appliances, even the alarm system, can be controlled with one tap. Design the user interface for a dedicated touch screen for home automation.

User demand
-The need to have an onboarding
- Intuitive and easy to use design
- More automation options
- Schedules / Timers for the diferent devices
- Notifications letting you know when the devices were left on and information to help save energy

Persona
Mary, 32 years old, married to John, has 2 small children and due to her busy lifestyle and the lack of time, she needs a app where she can help her control the lights, climate, air conditioning and appliances of the house, while also helping her saving more energy.

User flow
The darker gray represents the main menu

Color Palette
Gradients using bright colors for grabbing more attention with the main colors being:
Blue - viewed as trustworthy and cool
Green - view as warm and inviting, lending customers a pleasing feeling
