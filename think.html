Think it, Post it!

Branding developed for a discipline in the Degree of Design and Multimedia where the aim was to develop a branding for a product/app/etc that other colleague would imagine and brief us about the idea. The project that I had in hands was an app that would help organize photos, lists, memos, etc.. and where you could not only store your own things in the app but also have a profile. Having this in mind I developed the brand and some mockups showing the desktop app.
