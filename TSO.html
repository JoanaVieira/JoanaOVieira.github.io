TSO Jewellery

New branding for the facebook store “The Special One Jewellery“ done in collaboration with another design student. We were given full liberty for exploring and designing the brand. Since this brand was for a more feminine target we decided to focus on the colors like dark pink and orange. The final result was obtained by overlapping four circles two of them with the same color and by adding transparencies to each circle.
